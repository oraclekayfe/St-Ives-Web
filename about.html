About St Ives Specialist Hospital

St. Ives Specialist Hospital is a leading private hospital group in Nigeria.

Founded in 1996 in a 3-bedroom apartment in Ikeja, we have grown into a highly revered multi-specialist healthcare provider operating from 4 branches in Ikoyi, Ikeja, Akowonjo in Lagos State, and in Ibarra, Abeokuta in Ogun State.

Ives is dedicated to three specific practices: women health, children health, and family health with four distinct healthcare operations.

operational arm is situated at separate locations, well equipped with modern facilities, and well-staffed with qualified specialists and professionals who are abreast of all modern trends, global best practices and developments in their various specialties.

We offer dedicated specialist services in all matters of women, children and family health, managed and delivered by a group of consultants in Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, Medicine and Surgery. St. Ives also provides referrals to affiliate medical experts abroad on occasions when required.

ST IVES SPECIALIST HOSPITAL

Our specialist practice provides first class maintenance and improvement of health via the reliable diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases, illnesses, injuries, and other physical and mental impairments. Our specialist hospitals are located in Ikoyi and Ikeja in Lagos.

ST IVES FAMILY CLINIC

Our family clinics provide the best first line of care for all members of the family using top of the range facilities and first-class family health physicians with the expertise and commitment to all aspects of family health issues relating to women, children and the men in their lives.

ST IVES FERTILITY UNIT

Dedicated to the provision of advanced IVF and fertility services for the attainment of pregnancy and conception needs of expectant couples at affordable costs. Our fertility unit started in 2007 and we boast one of the highest IVF success rates in the world with over 2000 successful IVF births in 10 years.

ST IVES PHARMACY

We operate modern pharmaceutical outlet, the SAINT STORE, retailing genuine, up to date, NAFDAC registered and industry approved drugs with the aim of ensuring our patients and stakeholders have trusted access to genuine medications. Our pharmacy operation is another testament of our corporate focus on TOTAL HEALTHCARE.
